Medical Testing Course All You Need To Know Before Joining

Dr. Uday Bhan Yadav (BAMS)

Updated Post: 01 July 2025

Medical Testing Course is one of the branches of Science courses, which includes experimentation and testing upon disease diagnosis, and act as mail role behind the medical world. Whenever we go to visit a doctor for check up, he/she needs information about the parts, organs, and biochemical components within our body, to diagnose the fault, for medication. 

It may be any type of fracture, tumor, hemorrhage, or dysfunction of any organ or component of our body, we need medical testing from medical laboratories to diagnose and confirm it. So, for this medical testing process, we need skilled people who have been specifically prepared for the including processes.

What are included in Medical Testing course OR Medical Laboratory Technology?

  • CMLT : Certificate in Medical Laboratory Technology Course 
  • DMLT : Diploma in Medical Laboratory Technology Course 
  • BMLT : Bachelors in Medical Laboratory Technology Course 
  • MMLT : Masters in Medical Laboratory Technology Course 

 Which departments are to be included in Medical Testing Course?

  • The subjects or departments related to applied biological sciences group, which are related to medical course to most extent, are included in Medical Testing Course.
  • Subjects dealing with blood-sputum-saliva-urine testing, tissue culture, Hormones testing, Biochemical testing, Biopsy, ELISA, PCR, etc. are studied in Medical Testing Course.

Why to study Medical Testing Course?

  • According to recent surveys, the need for biomedical sciences have grown immensely, and the post-corona era has surged the recruitment of Medical Testing course graduates with a steep progression from 30%-40%.
