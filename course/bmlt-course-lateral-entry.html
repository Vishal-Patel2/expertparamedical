BMLT Course Lateral Entry

BMLT course(Lateral entry) helps students who have finished their Diploma in Medical Laboratory program can transition straight into the second year of the BMLT course via this course. This course offers learners the chance to bypass the first year and instantly enter the second year, thus saving money and time of the student.

After finishing your DMLT course, you have the opportunity to enrol in one of Bhadohi's leading Paramedical colleges the Expert Paramedical Institute & Research Center.

Eligibility Criteria

  • The students are selected on a Merit Basis, It requires a minimum of 55% marks one should have secured in the BSc in Medical Laboratory Technology (BMLT).

Career opportunities

Many different job opportunities would enable you to work as a professional in the following fields some of them are:

 1. Medical Laboratory Technologist/Technician

Conduct laboratory tests, analyze results, and assist in diagnosing diseases.

 2. Clinical Laboratory Supervisor/Manager

    Oversee the operations of a laboratory, including managing staff, ensuring quality control, and handling administrative tasks.

 3. Laboratory Quality Assurance Specialist

    Ensure that laboratory procedures comply with regulations and standards, and improve testing accuracy and reliability.

 4. Biomedical Scientist

    Conduct experiments and research to improve diagnostic and therapeutic techniques, and contribute to scientific advancements in medicine.

 5. Research and Development

    Work in developing new testing methods, diagnostic tools, or therapeutic solutions.

 6. Educational and Training Roles

    Teach and train future laboratory technologists and technicians in academic institutions or through professional development programs.

 7. Public Health Laboratories

    Work in public health settings to monitor and control diseases, and contribute to health policy and epidemiological studies.

 8. Forensic Laboratories

    Analyze biological and chemical samples related to criminal investigations and legal cases.

 9. Sales and Marketing in Laboratory Equipment and Reagents

    Promote and sell laboratory equipment, diagnostic tools, and reagents to healthcare facilities and research institutions.

 10. Health Informatics

    Integrate and manage health data systems, analyze data trends, and support decision-making in healthcare settings.
