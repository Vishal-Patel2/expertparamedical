LABORATORY

A Medical Laboratory Internship is an obligatory period of experiential learning spanning 24 weeks where the graduate of the Biomedical Sciences/Medical Laboratory Sciences is trained in the clinical diagnostic laboratory to acquire the knowledge, skills, and competencies required to function as an independent Medical Laboratory Technologist.

This internship period is mandatory for graduates of Biomedical Sciences/Medical Laboratory Sciences to take the Medical Laboratory Technologist Licensing examination of the Ministry of Health and Prevention.

Expert Paramedical Institute & Research Center is offering an Internship program. laboratories are approved for medical laboratory internship by the Ministry of Health and Prevention, BhadohiNCR. expert Diagnostic Labs is a national chain of ultramodern, comprehensive, fully automated laboratories in Rohini, Yamuna Vihar, Nagloi, and Mangolpuri. 

The Medical Laboratory internship program is offered every year. The fall batch internship starts in the first week of September and the Spring batch in the first week of March every year. There are limited seats, so students are instructed to apply well in advance.

Aims of the internship

The internship program at Expert Paramedical Institute & Research Center aims to

  • Nourish ‘on the job’ experience under close supervision.
  • Provide opportunities to attain appropriate higher skills and competencies before entering the workplace as laboratory/research/industry personnel.
  • Give an insight into the practice of the chosen profession
  • Serve as a strong motivating aspect for the student to resume and specialize additionally in the chosen field of study.
  • Provide an essential functional link between the educational program and joining the workforce.

Eligibility for Internship

  • Certificate or Diploma in Medical Lab Technology.
  • Bachelor of BSc in Biomedical, Bachelor of Science in Medical Laboratory Sciences degree or equivalent

How to Apply?

Fill online Application Form

The required documents are as part of the internship application:

  • Updated 2-page CV listing your details, educational qualifications, career goals, your interests, achievements
  • 2 Letters of Recommendation
  • Letter of Good Standing
  • College / University Transcripts
  • Final Degree or Provisional Degree Certificate
  • Valid passport with valid BhadohiNCR visa (For local: Family Book)
  • Valid Government ID
  • Birth Certificate
  • 4 Recent Passport size photo (white background)
  • Medical Fitness Certificates (Vaccination) like BCG, HBV, and Tetanus are mandatory.

Which degree is best for the laboratory?

Is radiology easy to study?

Radiology continues to be an excellent field in medicine. The job is engaging and requires intellectual stimulation. The task is frequently extensive and profound (numerous abnormalities in numerous modalities) yet can also be specialized in specific areas.

Which is best MLT or MLS?

MLS is perfect for those interested in the scientific principles behind laboratory testing and seeking a basis for future academic pursuits or experimentation. Bachelor of Science MLT is suitable for those who like to be hands-on and aim to become skilled in conducting different lab procedures after finishing their studies.

Is lab technician a good career?

India has a high career growth potential for Medical lab technicians. Individuals are enrolling in a B.sc MLT Course to advance to higher positions like Laboratory Manager or Head of Department. They are also able to transition into roles in research or teaching.

Can MLT open a laboratory?

Certainly, upon finishing a postgraduate degree in Medical Laboratory Technology (MLT), you have the opportunity to establish your pathology laboratory. Obtaining a Master of Science in Medical Laboratory Technology provides individuals with the essential expertise and abilities needed in laboratory sciences and diagnostics.
