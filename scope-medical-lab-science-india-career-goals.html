Updated Post: 17 May 2025

What is Medical Lab Science?

Medical lab science is a branch of science which gives you a chance to explore the career in the medical field on a diagnosis level. This field is related to who might be interested in science, but also in technology because it‚Äôs connected to various medical related factors. Such as:

  • Diagnosis
  • Equipment
  • Medical  Procedure

Medical lab science (MLS) also known as medical lab technology. If you pursue this health profession you would develop into medical lab technician, medical laboratory scientist etc.

What is the Role of Medical Lab Science (MLS)?

MLS have a crucial role in healthcare world like the detectives in the outside world. They would look for clues by engaging in the various diagnosis tools for the following:

  • Disease Diagnosis
  • Treatment Planning
  • Monitoring of the Disease

MLS contribute to the field of healthcare. The key role of the MLS across different areas is as follows:

  1. Blood Banking  and Transfusion Medicine
  2. Infectious Disease Control
  3. Genetic Testing
  4. Clinical Chemistry
  5. Hematology
  6. Immunology And Serology
  7. Microbiology
  8. Molecular Diagnostics
  9. Research Support
  10. Quality Assurance

What is the eligibility requirement for medical lab science course?

Expert Paramedical Institute & Research Center is study centre to provide this course and this course is affiliated by the prestigious university of Bir Tikendrajit. 

Eligibility

The individual must have completed 10+2 from a recognized board with a minimum aggregate of 50%. In other words, the student needs to have graduated from high school with at least

50% of the possible points in the relevant qualifying exam from an accredited educational board. PCB (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology) must have been studied by the students as requires coursework in Class 12 or through any other qualifying examination that is comparable.

What are the career opportunities in Medical Lab Science?

Expert Paramedical Institute & Research Center provides you with various opportunities after completing this course. An individual can work in the various healthcare settings, including the following:

  • Hospitals
  • Clinics
  • Research Laboratories
  • Non Funded Agency 
  • Diagnostics Centres

Outcomes of the Course

The expected outcomes of this course are as follows:

  1. Application of the learnt knowledge and technical skills.
  2. Application of problem resolving techniques to aid in the following:
  • Recognize and correct technical errors
  • Recognize instrument faults 
  • Seek appropriate supervisory assistance
  • Authenticate accuracy of laboratory results
  1. Work as a leader in the assorted professional field and industrial research zones. Connect the field of science and nature to work, and present yourself with confidence to the outside world by means of oral, written and technical communication
